Only Golden Cross sends live Slack alerts
Resistance Break, Higher Highs + Higher Lows, and Channel Break are still computed every night (feeds the Backtest tab and the colored circle markers on ticker charts), but they were intentionally removed from live Slack alerting on 2026-05-29 — backtest data showed no consistent return edge measured from the signal's fire date for those three (only Golden Cross held up: 87% win rate, avg +22.6% at 90d from fire). If it feels like triggers have gone quiet, this is why — it's a deliberate narrowing, not a break. Golden Cross alerts are still firing normally (see history below).

Live Alert Criteria — Golden Cross
Qualifying Insider Buy
Open-market buy (code P) · not a 10b5-1 sale/plan trade · value ≥ ${{ '{:,.0f}'.format(signal_scan_min_value) }} · traded within the last {{ signal_scan_lookback_days }} days
Signal Timing
50-day MA must cross above the 200-day MA within 15 days of the trade date, and that crossover must still be fresh — fired within the last {{ signal_scan_max_signal_age_days }} days
Deduplication
One alert per (issuer, fire date). If several insiders bought ahead of the same crossover, the largest qualifying buy is used as the representative trade in the message.
System Requirements
